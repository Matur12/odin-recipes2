Sweet Potatoes

Sweet Potatoes

Feeling like fries but don't want to have oily, unhealthy ones?

Sweet potatoes from the oven are just right for you

Ingredients

  • 2 big sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • potato spice,salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the sweet potatoes into fry size
  2. Set oven to 180 degrees Celsius with hot air
  3. Spread out the cut fries over the oven plate
  4. Add spices and olive oil all over the plate
  5. Let it sit in the oven for about 30 min. Check if needed to stay longer
  6. Serve with your favourite sauce (mayonaise, obviously)
