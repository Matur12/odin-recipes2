Indian curry

Chicken Curry

A delicious, vegan meal that will satisfy your appetite and your tastebuds! Once you make curry, you'll never be in a hurry!

A dish originally from India, it has long since made its way onto the plates of people everywhere around the world.

Ingredients

  • Jasmin Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Coconut cream
  • 400 gram frozen veggies to your choice
  • 1 whole onion
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • Curry powder
  • salt
  • 200 gram chicken filet

Steps

  1. Boil jasmin rice in a separate pot according to instructions
  2. Cut chicken in smaller pieces and stirfry in a pan with olive oil
  3. Chop onion and garlic in small pieces, add to chicken
  4. Add curry powder (be royal with it)
  5. Once chicken is goldbrown, add frozen veggies
  6. When all contents are hot, add coconut cream to liquify. Add salt and other spices to taste
